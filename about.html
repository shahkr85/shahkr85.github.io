About Me

Hello! My name is Krutik Shah. I am an Electrical & Computer Engineering major, at Rowan University, Class of 2023! I am passionate about PCB design & assembly, 3D printing, robotics, aerospace and aeronautics!

Biography

I was born in Gujarat, India on April 10, 2000. I moved to the US in 2007 with my parents, raised in Flemington, NJ where I went to Hunterdon Central Regional High School. There at the high school, I was a part of the FIRST Robotics Team (FRC) Team 3637, where I developed a passion for engineering. I would not be where I am today without the experience and knowledge I gained through that team. I was also a student worker for school's IT department. After applying to my colleges, I decided to go to Rowan University for an ECE degree, where I was a student worker for the ECE department, working in the Resource Center, Fabrication Center, and Assembly Center.
Currently, I am working on a research project for the Advanced Materials & Manufacturing Institute (AMMI), at the South Jersey Technology Park where I am helping develop a composite DLP printer. I am also currently the Vice President of the American Institute of Aeronautics & Astronautics (AIAA), the student branch at Rowan University.
